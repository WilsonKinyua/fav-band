Who are H_ART THE BAND?

At the end of 2012, MORDECAI KIMEU auditioned alongside KENNETH MUYA and WACHIRA GATAMA to establish H Art the Band. Mordecai auditioned Kenchez and Wachira for a performance on cancer awareness at the Kenya National Theatre in late 2012, and the three met in the corridors of their performing careers. During rehearsals, the three learned that they shared a love of music, spoken word/poetry, comedy, dancing, fashion, and life in general. H ART the BAND was founded not long after, on the 22nd of February 2013, when the three performed together for the first time at that Ethiopian eatery.

With both Mordecai and Wachira having been crowned Slam Africa Kings, H_ART’s music is heavily inspired by spoken word poetry. Self taught guitarist Kenchez compliments the rhymes with smooth, danceable tunes and some undeniable Luhya swag. Everywhere H_ART performs, they leave a lasting impression.

For the following reasons, I would always choose them over any other band:

  • Musical Style - Their musical style has been described as abstract, African-pop, urban-colored vintage, uniquely hilarious, HAIR band..."to mention a few phrases used to describe this diverse group of artists.
  • Their Inspiration - H ART's music is highly influenced by everyday life. With Mordecai and Wachira both being "Slam Africa Kings," Kenchez, a self-taught guitarist, complements their rhymes with smooth, danceable tunes, while Mordecai wows their jams with his heavenly vocals and lyrics at seemingly calculated moments, this fusion creates a fresh new style they like to call "AFROPOETRY."
