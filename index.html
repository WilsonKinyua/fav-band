Who are Sauti Sol?

Bien-Aim√© Baraza, Willis Chimano, and Savara Mudigi created the Kenyan afro-pop band Sauti Sol in Nairobi, Kenya in 2005. Polycarp Otieno, a guitarist, joined the group before they changed their name to Sauti Sol.

Mwanzo, Sauti Sol's debut studio album, was released on November 1, 2008, to critical acclaim. Sol Filosofia, their second studio album, was released on February 25, 2011, and received numerous honors and nominations. The band collaborated with South African rapper and song producer Spoek Mathambo to create a self-titled extended play on June 18, 2012.

The band has completed several successful tours in Africa and Europe, topped Kenyan charts, and gained international attention with shows in Europe and the United States, as well as television appearances and numerous accolades and nominations, including a concert performance in Kenya in 2011 with Ladysmith Black Mambazo, a South African a cappella group.
