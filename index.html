Who are Sauti Sol?

Bien-Aimé Baraza, Willis Chimano, and Savara Mudigi created the Kenyan afro-pop band Sauti Sol in Nairobi, Kenya in 2005. Polycarp Otieno, a guitarist, joined the group before they changed their name to Sauti Sol.

Mwanzo, Sauti Sol's debut studio album, was released on November 1, 2008, to critical acclaim. Sol Filosofia, their second studio album, was released on February 25, 2011, and received numerous honors and nominations. The band collaborated with South African rapper and song producer Spoek Mathambo to create a self-titled extended play on June 18, 2012.

The band has completed several successful tours in Africa and Europe, topped Kenyan charts, and gained international attention with shows in Europe and the United States, as well as television appearances and numerous accolades and nominations, including a concert performance in Kenya in 2011 with Ladysmith Black Mambazo, a South African a cappella group.

Why the Sauti Sol?

For the following reasons, I would always choose them over any other band:

  • Musical Style - Their musical style has been described as abstract, African-pop, urban-colored vintage, uniquely hilarious.
  • Their Inspiration - Sauti Sol's music is highly influenced by everyday life.
  • Fusion of Poetry and Music - Various Kenyan Poets that have explored other kinds of poetry performance not with the same success as Sauti Sol.

SAUTI SOL MEMBERS

Willis Austin Chimano

Willis Austin Chimano is a member of the popular all-boy band Sauti Sol and is a vocalist, keytarist, and saxophonist.

Savara Mudigi

Savara Mudigi is a Grammy Award - winning Singer, Songwriter, Music producer at Sauti Sol.

Polycarp Otieno

Polycarp Otieno is a famous Guitarist. Kenyan guitarist and producer of the popular music group Sauti Sol who are best known for albums such as Mwanzo and Sol Filosofia.

Bien-Aimé Baraza

Bien-Aimé Baraza is songwriter, Guitar, Piano and also a member of Sauti Sol.
